Downtown Cairo
Historic, walkable, lively.

Kazaz for shawarma on the go; Felfela for classic mezze; Room Art Space for live music coffee breaks.

How to book

Most spots take calls or WhatsApp; upscale venues often use Resy-like forms on their site. For seaside places, ask for outdoor seating.

Peak times

Thursday and Friday nights are busiest in Cairo/Alex; sunsets fill up Nile and seaside terraces. Reserve 24-48 hours ahead.

Local tips

Ask for tap water if you do not want bottled, and check service fees on the bill (often included). Tipping 10-12% is appreciated.
